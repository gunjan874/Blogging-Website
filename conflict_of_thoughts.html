Conflict of Thoughts

Generally, we all have a busy schedule. We are occupied in our lives and always try to fix the unpredictable puzzle. We aspire for recognition. If someone acknowledges our work and dealings, that makes us happy. We perceive ourselves as the only doer of good things and if something odd happens, at times we are in a state of mental conflict. I would define this as an act of not being benevolent towards our nature for whatever it offers. It can be a chocolate which we adore but on the contrary it can also be a bitter medicine. Many times, bitterness adds sugar and gives us the best results. To quote this instance, let's go back to the phase we all wished to never haunt us again - “The period in Lockdown”. We were at our homes, struggling for our breaths. Every breath was precious. There was a war in the nation to save our loved ones against the virus. With time the bitterness diffused and the sweetness overpowered the nation.

The steps one takes in professional as well as in personal life are all dependent on the stability of the state of mind. If someone is hasty in decision making and always gets perplexed then there are high chances to take the wrong path which may not be the desired goal of one’s life. The state of war within the brain, between the thoughts produces a product which may or may not be efficient and required.

Conflict can’t only be between countries for fuel and energy, but today, the conflict is only for technology. Every country aims to be at the best position. To reach the peak, they perform bad deeds which is not at all judicious for both the parts.

In nutshell, the circumstances depend only on the decision making power of an individual. At times, the effects can be adverse but it can also be rewarding if an individual is composed and makes a verdict generously.
